✅ ENHANCED PREDICTION AGGREGATOR
   File: src/footy/prediction_aggregator.py (enhanced)
   
   New mathematical methods:
   
   1. Bayesian Model Averaging (BMA)
      - Computes posterior probabilities from historical accuracy
      - Weight_k ∝ exp(accuracy_k × 2.0)
      - More principled than fixed weights
      - Sharpens differences between model accuracies
   
   2. Jensen-Shannon Divergence
      - jensen_shannon_divergence(p, q): Symmetric KL divergence
      - Bounded in [0, 1]: 0=identical, 1=totally different
      - Used for measuring expert disagreement
   
   3. Model Agreement from JS Divergences
      - model_agreement_from_divergences(): Pairwise JS computation
      - Overall agreement = 1 - mean_divergence
      - Better than std() of probabilities alone
   
   4. Enhanced Value Edge Computation
      - Confidence weighting: edge × model_confidence
      - Optional odds conversion for fine-grained comparison
      - Converts decimal odds to implied probabilities
      - Removes overround for fair comparison
      - Bounded output [-1, 1]
   
   5. Multi-Signal Upset Risk
      - 5 signals with learned weights:
        * 25% - Market/model disagreement
        * 20% - Favorite weakness
        * 25% - Internal model disagreement
        * 15% - Expert prediction spread (std)
        * 15% - Low overall confidence
      - Outputs [0, 1] risk score
   
   6. Enhanced aggregate_predictions()
      - Parameters: use_bayesian_averaging, expert_accuracies, market_odds
      - Supports both BMA and legacy LogOP
      - Computes JS-based model agreement
      - Returns richer UnifiedPrediction

4. MATHEMATICAL FOUNDATION
================================================================================

TrueSkill:
  - Reference: Herbrich et al. (2006) "TrueSkill: A Bayesian Skill Rating System"
  - Bayesian hierarchical model with Gaussian skills
  - Factor graph inference (simplified Kalman filter)

Bayesian State-Space:
  - Reference: Koopman & Lit (2015) "Dynamic bivariate Poisson model"
  - Rue & Salvesen (2000) "Prediction and retrospective analysis of soccer"
  - Dynamic Bayesian networks with shrinkage priors

Model Aggregation:
  - Reference: Genest & Zidek (1986) "Combining probability distributions"
  - Logarithmic Opinion Pool (theoretically optimal)
  - Bayesian Model Averaging from accuracy

Jensen-Shannon Divergence:
  - Reference: Jensen & Shannon (1991) "Divergence and Sufficiency"
  - Symmetric, bounded divergence measure
  - Foundation for model agreement metrics

Conformal Prediction:
  - Reference: Vovk et al. (2005) "Algorithmic Learning in a Random World"
  - Guaranteed coverage (90% intervals contain truth 90% of time)
  - Distribution-free approach
